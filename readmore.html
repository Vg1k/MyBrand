The story of Urus

The Lamborghini Urus is a high-performance SUV that embodies the Italian automaker's tradition of extreme luxury, breathtaking design, and exhilarating performance. It's powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine that delivers 641 horsepower, which can take it from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.5 seconds, and up to a top speed of 190 mph. The Urus also features an advanced all-wheel-drive system, adaptive air suspension, and carbon-ceramic brakes, ensuring exceptional handling and stability on any terrain. The cabin is spacious, stylish, and loaded with high-tech features, making the Lamborghini Urus an impressive blend of performance, luxury, and practicality.
